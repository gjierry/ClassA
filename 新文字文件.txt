Information technology is a general term for various technologies mainly used to manage and process information. It mainly applies computer science and communication technology to design, develop, install and deploy information systems and application software. According to the differences in storing and processing information, the development of information technology can be divided into several different stages: pre-mechanical period, mechanical period, electromechanical period, and electronic period.
